Welcome to XYN Frameless Glass & Epoxy Flooring, where we specialise in providing high-quality solutions for both residential and commercial properties.

Our range of products includes frameless glass balustrades, luxury showers, glass doors, front facades, patio enclosures, stacking systems, and premium epoxy flooring.

Established in 1999 and headed by Midas Ndamase, XYN Frameless Glass is a Close Corporation with over 25 years of experience in structural delivery. We conceptualize strategies and genuine tools that empower our clientele through excellence.

Our frameless glass products and epoxy floors are designed to enhance the aesthetics and functionality of any space, providing a sleek and modern look that seamlessly blends with the surrounding architectural environment.

At XYN, we are committed to providing our customers with exceptional service and high-quality products, maintaining a high standard of quality in every project.

Our team of experienced professionals, led by Midas Ndamase, will work closely with you to understand your specific needs and design a customised solution.

From concept to final installation, we guide you through every step of the process, ensuring all projects are completed on time and within budget.
