Welcome to our company, where we specialise in providing high-quality frameless glass solutions for both residential and commercial properties.

Our range of products includes frameless glass balustrades, luxury showers, glass doors, front facades, cellars, and stacking systems.

Established in 1999 and headed by Midas Ndamase, Xinyanyani Projects is a Close Corporation with over 25 years of experience in structural delivery. We conceptualize strategies and genuine tools that empower our clientele through excellence.

Our frameless glass products are designed to enhance the aesthetics and functionality of any space, providing a sleek and modern look that seamlessly blends with the surrounding architectural environment.

At our company, we are committed to providing our customers with exceptional service and High-quality products, maintaining a high standard of quality in every project.

Our team of experienced professionals, led by Midas Ndamase, will work closely with you to understand your specific needs and design a customised structural solution.

From concept to final installation, we guide you through every step of the process, ensuring all projects are completed on time and within budget.
