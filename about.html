About Us

Discover the legacy behind XYN Frameless Glass & Epoxy Flooring

Our Legacy

Established in 1999 and headed by Midas Ndamase, XYN Frameless Glass & Epoxy Flooring is a Close Corporation with over 25 years of experience in structural delivery. We conceptualize strategies and genuine tools that empower our clientele through excellence.

Our frameless glass products and epoxy floors are designed to enhance the aesthetics and functionality of any space, providing a sleek and modern look that seamlessly blends with the surrounding architectural environment.

From residential homes to commercial properties, we have successfully completed thousands of installations across Gauteng, earning a reputation for reliability, precision, and exceptional craftsmanship.
